TCC background

Initiated at the inception of close international cooperation between China and South Africa, The Cellar finds its foundation in promoting the interests of theses nations, not only in trade, but viticultural history. To share in each others traditions and pride is more than a meeting of glasses but a meeting of generations.

该酒窖始建于中国与南非密切国际合作之初，在贸易与在葡萄栽培历史方面，提升中国和南非的多重利益。分享各国的传统和宝藏，这不仅是红酒杯的邂逅，更是世代的相遇。
